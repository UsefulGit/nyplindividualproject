Welcome. What is exactly is “Apokriesphilia,” you ask? An excellent question. It is an invented word formed by combining the Greek word for Halloween,“Απόκριες,”(Apόkries) with the Greek “philia” suffix, which means“a love of.” Therefore, it means “the love of Halloween.” How is Απόκριες pronounced?
