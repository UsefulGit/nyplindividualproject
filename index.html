What is exactly is "Apokriesphilia", you ask? An excellent question. It is a made-up word formed by combining the Greek word for Halloween, "Απόκριες," with the Greek "philia" suffix, which means "a love of." If you'd like to hear how Απόκριες is pronounced, click here. Therefore, it means "the love of Halloween."
